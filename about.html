About Wooyoung Tech

Wooyoung Tech Co., Ltd. was established with a single mission: to help farmers grow more with less. We design and manufacture a complete range of irrigation products — from individual drip emitters to full turnkey systems — that increase crop yields, conserve precious water resources, and reduce operational costs.

Our R&D team continuously develops next-generation solutions integrating sensor technology, AI-driven scheduling, and sustainable materials, ensuring our customers stay ahead in an era of climate uncertainty.
