Product

Wooyoung Tech drip tape operates optimally at 0.5–1.5 kgf/cm² (50–150 kPa). Within this range, a uniform flow rate is guaranteed across the entire length of the tape.

Drippers deliver water drop-by-drop directly to the root zone, minimizing evaporation and surface runoff. Mini sprinklers distribute water over a radius of 1–4 m in a spray pattern. The best choice depends on your crop type and growing environment.

Under normal operating conditions, Wooyoung Tech drip tape lasts 3–5 years. Proper UV protection and consistent filtration can extend service life further.

We recommend cleaning filters at least three times per season — before, during, and after irrigation. If the pressure drop across the filter exceeds 0.2 kgf/cm², clean immediately regardless of the schedule.

Cool-fog systems are especially effective in greenhouses growing heat-sensitive crops such as tomatoes, peppers, lettuce, flowers, and mushrooms. They provide simultaneous cooling and precise humidity control.
